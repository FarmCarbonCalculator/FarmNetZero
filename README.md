# FarmNetZero
GHG emissions and sequestration data from Farm Net Zero project 2021-2026

Footprints were typically generated in 2022 & 2026 for the preceding year 

The farms reported in were all located in Cornwall, and whole farm footprints were untaken with the support of trained advisors.

The majority of farmers gave permission for thier anonymised data to be shared, those who did not consent had their data withdrawn. 

This data is the output of the Farm Net Zero project, kindly funded by ... example change
